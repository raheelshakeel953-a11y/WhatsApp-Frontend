Welcome to WhatsApp. With WhatsApp, you’ll get fast, simple, and secure messaging and calling available on phones all over the world. To get started, download WhatsApp for free from the
Google Play Store.

Scan the QR code with your phone's camera and tap the link to be taken to the WhatsApp download page on the Play Store.

Set up WhatsApp

Learn how to verify your phone number in this article and how to add contacts in this article. Once you’ve set up WhatsApp on your phone, you can choose to use WhatsApp on multiple devices.

Linked Devices

Linked devices provide a reliable, secure way to access WhatsApp from any of your devices. You can stay connected by linking up to four devices at a time to your primary phone. You’ll still need your primary phone to register your WhatsApp account and link new devices.

    Supported linked devices include:
  • Windows
  • Mac
  • Web
  • Android tablet
  • WearOS
  • Companion Phones
  • Ray-Ban Stories and Ray-Ban Meta
  • VR
